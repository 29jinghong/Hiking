About Our Journey

We are a group of friends — setting out on our very first mountain adventures.
Some of us have never climbed a trail before. Others just want to feel the wind, the trees, and the sky beyond the screen.
Together, we're exploring Georgia's hills, canyons, and high ridges.

This site is where we map our journey. Not just the places we visit, but the memories we make: the shared laughter, the breathtaking views, the quiet moments around the campfire.

If you're reading this maybe you're just starting too. That's perfect. Let's climb slowly, wander far, and enjoy life, together.
